\int_0^1 v(t)dt & = & \int_0^{0.2} v(t)dt + \int_{0.2}^{0.6} v(t)dt + \int_{0.6}^{0.8} v(t)dt + \int_{0.8}^1 v(t)dt \\

&\approx & h_1 \frac{v(0) + v(0.2)}{2} + \darkGreen{h_2 \frac{v(0.2) + v(0.6)}{2}} + \\

\end{eqnarray*}
}

\[
h_1 = 0.2, \quad h_2 = 0.4, \quad h_3 = 0.2, \quad h_4 = 0.2
